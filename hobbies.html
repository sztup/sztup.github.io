Outdoor Activities

Winter Hiking

I love hiking in challenging conditions, it's fun, and I really enjoy learning and using new skills. As a Winter Hiking Leader at MIT Outing Club (MITOC), I lead groups in the White Mountains. It's an amazing experience learning from the other leaders and introducing new people to winter hiking and convincing them that hiking below 5F can be a great experience.
