Research Interests

My research integrates computational methods with clinical insights to advance cancer treatment:

  • • Developing novel computational methods to identify DNA repair deficiencies in cancer
  • • Creating machine learning approaches for biomarker discovery and patient stratification
  • • Investigating the role of immune responses in cancer treatment
  • • Translating genomic insights into clinical applications

Research

My research aims to identify DNA repair deficiencies and develop companion diagnostics through computational approaches. Key achievements include:

  • Developed a bioinformatics method to estimate homologous recombination deficiency (cited in over 160 publications)
  • Identified HR- and NER-deficient prostate, bladder, and ovarian cancers
  • Led research in oncoimmunology, exploring gut microbiome's role in checkpoint inhibitor therapy
